AngularJS Window Resize

Resize your browser window.

Resized: {{resizeCounter}} 
(function() {
    'use strict';

    angular.module('myApp', ['resize']);

    angular.module('myApp').controller('ResizeCtrl', ['$scope', 'resize', '$timeout', function($scope, resize, $timeout) {
        $scope.resizeCounter = 0;

        resize.init({
            scope: $scope,
            resizedFn: function() {
                $scope.resizeCounter++;
            },
            debounce: 200
        });
    }]);
})();

* Why is $scope in the options? $scope is needed to unbind the resize listener when scope is destroyed so that the event does not leak into undesired areas of your application.
